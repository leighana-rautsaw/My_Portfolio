My Design Process

In my design process, I value and implement the double diamond method due it’s great framework to structure or plan a project. When identifying a problem and developing a solution, the double diamond method is an excellent tool for understanding design. The framework for this methodology encourages creativity and innovation while focusing on the core issue & its impact on users.

In my own design process, I follow this method using divergent and convergent thinking to immerse myself in the problem through research & generate potential solutions. Once you understand how it works, you start seeing problems & solutions everywhere.
